A Digital Khata App for Pakistani Shopkeepers

Credit Keeper is a lightweight, secure Flutter application designed to replace traditional paper-based ledger books (Khata). It allows shopkeepers to manage customer debts (Credit) and payments (Zama) with ease, offering a professional digital receipt system.

‚ú® Features
Customer Management: Add, search, and manage customer profiles.

Digital Ledger: Record credit (Udhaar) and payments (Payment) instantly.

Multi-Select Delete: Efficiently manage records by deleting multiple customers or transactions at once.

Quick Add: Record transactions directly from the dashboard for frequent customers.

Professional Receipts: Generate text-based receipts in PKR (Rs.) and share them via any messaging app.

Business Insights: View total market credit and net balance at a glance.

Customizable: Personalize the app with your own Shop Name (e.g., KNB Mart).
